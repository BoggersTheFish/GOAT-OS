# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

- Be respectful and inclusive. Welcome newcomers and differing experience levels.
- Focus on constructive feedback. Critique ideas, not people.
- Report harassment or unacceptable behavior; maintainers will address it.

## Scope

Applies to the project’s spaces: repo, issues, PRs, and any official communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit content, warn, or temporarily/indefinitely ban accounts for behavior that violates these standards. Serious issues may be reported to GitHub or other appropriate bodies.

## Attribution

This code of conduct is adapted from common open-source community standards to keep BTFOS/GOAT-OS a constructive place for collaboration.
